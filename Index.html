About The College



Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad, University College of Engineering Jagtial(JNTUHUCEJ) was established in the year 2007 , Jagtial with 5 different branches viz,IT,CSE ME,EEE AND ECE.JNTUHUCEJ aims to be an institute of excellence in training the student to meet the global needs and is supported by highly qualified and well experienced professors drawn from research and industry background.The location of the sprawling campus surrounded by lush green hills ,temples and academic ambience uploads the traditional and culture values among the students.

About The Department

The Department of Information Technology is offering B.Tech & M.Tech(IT) courses with an intake of 66 and 18 students each respectively.The department is equipped with furnished laboratories.The faculty members are highly qualified with vast academic and industrial experience.Information Technology (IT) is a dynamic branch of technology focused on the management and processing of information through computers and telecommunications. It encompasses a broad range of disciplines including software development, hardware engineering, networking, cybersecurity, database management, and more. With rapid advancements in technology, IT plays a crucial role in shaping modern society, revolutionizing industries, and enabling innovation across various sectors. It continues to evolve, driving efficiency, productivity, and connectivity in an increasingly digital world.



About the Symposium(CEZAR 2024)

JNTUH College of Engineering Jagtial (JNTUHUCEJ) was established in the year 2007 as a constituent college of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad. The college is recognized by UGC under Section 2(f) & 12 (B) of the UGC Act, 1956

CEZAR 2024 is an authority in generating globally accliamed Engineers.The mission behind laying the foundation is to strive in producing technically trained superior human resources to serve the present and future global needs by providing an excellent platform for interaction,knowledge sharing and talent showing.

Flash Mob

A flash mob is a spontaneous gathering of people in a public place to perform a brief, often choreographed, act or performance before quickly dispersing. These events are typically organized via social media or other online platforms and are intended to surprise and entertain unsuspecting bystanders.


Cult Night

"Cult night" typically refers to an event or gathering centered around the celebration or exploration of cult films, music, or other cultural phenomena. Participants often dress up as characters from cult classics, engage in themed activities, and indulge in shared appreciation for niche or underground cultural icons.
